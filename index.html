Welcome to endless rain.

This site is an exercise in self-expression. I've often forgotten about the "express" part — that thoughts and feelings mean little when they remain internal. Sartre says, "Il n'y a de réalité que dans l'action. (There is no reality except in action.)" Or worse, Freud says, "Unexpressed emotions will never die. They are buried alive and will come forth later in uglier ways." I'm going to try to avoid that.
